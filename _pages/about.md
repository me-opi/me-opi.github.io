Md Mehrab Hossain Opi is an incoming PhD student at Florida State University and currently an Assistant Professor at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology. His research focuses on High Performance Computing, Machine Learning efficiency optimization, sparse tensor algebra, and lossy data compression for scientific data. He works on improving computational efficiency and scalability for large-scale scientific and machine learning workloads, with an emphasis on sparse data structures and compression techniques that preserve scientific fidelity while reducing storage and compute costs.
