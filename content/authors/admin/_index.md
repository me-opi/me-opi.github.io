Md Mehrab Hossain Opi is a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET). His research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and High-Performance Computing, with a specific focus on resource-efficient model training and mixed-precision techniques for Bangla NLP. An avid competitive programmer, he represented his university as a World Finalist at the 48th ICPC World Finals in 2024. He is passionate about algorithms, teaching, and bridging the gap between theoretical computer science and practical AI applications.
